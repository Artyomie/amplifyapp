Vegan Crab Cakes with Arugula Salad

Ingredients

  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1 can hearts of palm
  • 1 can artichokes, or 2 of hearts of palm
  • 1 Â½ cups panko breadcrumbs, divided
  • 1/4 cup vegan mayonnaise
  • 1 tsp dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp seaweed salt
  • 1/2 tsp black or white pepper
  • 2 tbsp old bay seasoning
  • oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. Drain & rinse chickpeas, palms, and artichokes.
  2. Add them to a food processor and pulse until the mixture is chopped into chunky pieces.
  3. In a large bowl add ingredients above, along with 1 C bread crumbs, vegan mayo, dijon, lemon juice, salt, pepper, and old bay. Mix well by hand or with wooden soon.
  4. Form mixture into 1in thick patties and dip each one in remaining 1/4 C bread crumbs.
  5. When almost done with all the mixture start hearing a frying pan with as much oil as needed to 350F.
  6. Fry each patty for 4-6 mins per side or until golden.
  7. When finished plate with arugula salad below and drizzle with tartar sauce if desired.
