About

Hi, I'm Anyi Sanchez-Latypov!

I'm Dominican living in New Jersey, with an insatiable love for vegan cooking, plants, cats, and my supportive husband. I believe in making food for nurturing the soul, and doing so sustainably.

Here you can find many of my own delicious and easy to make vegan recipes, and my online store where you can by home-grown and hand-made products.

I am currently a student at Middlesex College training to be a professional chef. I love my cat Luna who is a little munchkin. My husband Artem made this website for me to show the world all my creations and share my love for plants and the environment
