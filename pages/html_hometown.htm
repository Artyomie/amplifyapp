Location

East Brunswick is a town in central New Jersey in Middlesex County that lies south of the Raritan river and is bisected by the New Jersey Turnpike. It is south of New Brunswick and is between North Brunswick and Sayreville. East Brunswick is about a twenty minute drive from Middlesex County College.

About

Population

East Brunswick has a growing population of 47,611 people as of 2019. It is a mixing pot of many different cultures since it is home to many immigrant communities, such as Russian and Jewish immigrants, a quarter of the population being from Asian decent.

Education

East Brunswick has a very good school system, being the only district in New Jersey with all schools achieving a "Blue Ribbon School" designation by the United States Department of Education. The high school is "East Brunswick High School"

Jobs

East Brunswick, like many of the regions nearby has a lot of commuters living here that have jobs in New York. East Brunswick, however, has a large pharmaceutical job sector, along with many healthcare workers working at the Rutgers hospitals and professors.

Things to Do

  • Shopping: In East Brunswick we have the East Brunswick square mall with many stores and a movie theater, with large department stores and also smaller stores from book stores to jewelry stores
  • Eating: There are many restaurants around here especially off of Route 18, and lot of Asian cuisine like Ichiban 18 or East Hana
  • Sports: East Brunswick has many different gyms all around, but it also has many parks with trails and open fields like Bicentennial park, or Rutgers Gardens
