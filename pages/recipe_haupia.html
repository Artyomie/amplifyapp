Haupia Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 can coconut milk 400ml
  • 4 tbsp sugar-5 tbsp
  • pinch salt
  • ½ cup water
  • 4 tbsp cornflour or arrowroot

Instructions

  1. Mix coconut milk, salt and sugar in a pan.
  2. Place it over medium heat and allow it to become hot till you see bubbles on the sides of the pan. In the meantime make the slurry by mixing cornflour or arrowroot flour with the water.
  3. When the coconut mixture becomes hot, stir it continously and slowly pour in the slurry. -Keep stirring till the mixture becomes thick and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  4. Pour the mixture into a tray. Use either a 8x6 or 8x8 or 6x6 tray.
  5. Bang the tray gently on the worktop to level out the mixture. Can even use a spatula to make the top smooth.
  6. When the mixture cools down a bit put the tray in the fridge to allow Haupia to set for about 2 hours.
  7. Cut the set Haupia into squares and serve.

Starfruit Lemon Gastrique

  • 2 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1/3-1/2 cup starfruit lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Mix sugar & water in pan, caramelize until lightly amber
  2. Gently heat juice
  3. Remove sugar mix from heat & slowly whisk juice
  4. Return to heat & reduce for 5-8 more mins.
  5. Cool slightly about 5 mins and pour on Haupia.
  6. (Optional) Garnish with ground hibiscus flower powder, and toasted coconut flakes.
